Overview

The Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG) in Rajasthan organizes the REAP (Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process), the gateway for ambitious students to pursue B.E/B.Tech and B.Arch courses in Rajasthan. This state-level process, supported by government-aided and private unaided technical institutions, offers a fair and competitive selection based on merit—no separate entrance exam required. Diploma holders in engineering and technology are also eligible. REAP 2025 features three rounds of counseling, open to candidates from Rajasthan and other states.

Eligibility Criteria for REAP 2025

For BE/ B.Tech Courses

  • Applicants must have completed 12th grade with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
  • Minimum 45% marks in qualifying exam (40% for ST/SC/PWD/OBC/SBC of Rajasthan).
  • Final exam appearing candidates are also eligible.

For B.Arch. Courses

  • Applicants must have completed or appeared in 12th grade with Mathematics as a compulsory subject.
  • Minimum 45% marks in qualifying exam.
  • Valid NATA score required for admission.
